Scribble Notes Features

Please follow the below steps to revoke access

Navigate to Google Drive settings

Go to the Google Drive Settings page and if you're not signed in please sign in with your Google Account used in our Scribble Notes. If you're not on settings page, then please go to your Google Drive and click on Settings (wheel icon) in the top right corner and select Settings option from the dropdown.
(refer below screenshot)

Manage Apps

At this point you should be able to see the settings page along with side-pan with few options like General, Notifications, Manage Apps.

Click on the third option and scroll down until you see our app name "Scribble Notes & Organizer" in the list and stop right there.

Revoke Access

When you see the app name in the list, stop scrolling. You would see Options dropdown button, click on to expand to see two options listed as

  • 1st option : Disconnect from Drive
  • 2nd option: Delete hidden app data - don't worry it hidden means it will not visible when you visit your mydrive.

Confirm or cancel

You would see the alert dialog with Confirm/Delete and Cancel actions to revoke the access with our application. Depends on the option you chose the information differs but down the line the goal is to revoke access.
