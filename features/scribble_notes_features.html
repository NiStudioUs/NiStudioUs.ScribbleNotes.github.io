Scribble Notes Features

Features that help you on everyday tasks.

Choose the right theme and colors for your eyes

We care for your eyes, so make use of the themes we provide. Everyone likes to have an options right, there are six wide range of colors along with font styles. Try them.

Choose your background

Who doesn't like to set their own photo as wallpaper, and you can choose to display on all screens or just login screen.

Secure Passcode

Uh oh, someone has my phone password, they might see the content inside.
Do not worry about when people have access codes, we here have unique passcode which you can choose, it might be your birthdate, vehicle number or fancy numbers. Decide how do you want to access the stored notes and tasks with either 6 digit passcode or skip passcode and login directly.

Skip passcode

Some likes to have an extra lock on their house, while some leave it wide open. Let us know who you're. So we decided to provide you an option to skip the passcode which lets you log in every time you (or) anyone who opens the app.
You can choose to skip the passcode during the set-up or anytime you want to skip as you go, this allows your notes visible to anyone, so be careful while you hand your phone to family members, friends or colleagues

We value your Privacy

This purely depends on your choice to give us access to provide the best features to you like the one below.

Permissions

Why not attaching images from the Gallery (or) Camera to support the content you have it in the notes as a reference.
You can always go back to app settings and revoke the permission as and when you wish.

Profile Information

Make use of our backup and restore option. We store the Google sign-in information in a secured location along with your username, email address and profile photo.
At a glance you should be able to know which account of users is being signed in with our app, So we display above-mentioned email, profile photo in Settings screen in Profile section (refer picture from right-hand side)

Opt-out

Anytime, when you feel like you have to opt-out from using our Backup and Restore (sync) service,
you're free to do by logout button and confirm in the alert dialog.
By opt-out your sign-in data from our app will be deleted but the data you backed up to your Google Drive will stay as it is. If you want to delete and revoke access by following the steps explained here

Group your Notes into folders

move and manage your contents anytime like how you manage you categorize your files. As and when you use our app you may ends-up with chunks of notes which becomes complex to manage, so we introduce the folders, yes you read right. You can create folders and create or move notes to the same. What else can you do, if the folder is temporary, you can just delete it.

Tasks Status At a glance

Who likes to track everything with text books, mark when it completed and count. So we decided to make it simple for you to know what is completed and what is not with powerful circular charts.

The detailed charts are here. Let me tell you a secret, you can interact with the legends/chart titles, when you touch them the detail fades away, touch back to retrieve and draw the chart again. Do you Know what is better, do not worry about how many tasks you have, just add and leave the rest to us, we choose how to split and display the stats for you.
